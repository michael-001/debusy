About us

Debusy Comfort Travels Limited is a technology and business solutions provider. Our passion is to conceptualize, design, deliver, and manage technology and business solutions that support our clients’ objectives. We strive to make our solutions and services simpler thereby ensuring our customers reap greater benefits. That is why we go ...beyond technology.

Hopperzlink has partnerships and strategic relationships with technology companies, e-payment switch and processing providers, and other relationships with original equipment manufacturers (OEMs) to provide cutting edge solutions to clients.

Hopperzlink was setup by a team of professionals & consultants with extensive experiences in technology and business consulting space having worked at Nigerian and international companies. Hopperzlink continues to expand the network of skilled and experienced personnel who are able to provide technical and professional services on wide range of technology projects.

Mission Statement

To provide innovative, agile and sustainable world class solutions.

Vision Statement

To be a leader and reference point in the delivery and management of simple and smart solutions for everyday business and living.

Core values

  • We are focused on expanding the frontiers of innovation
  • We challenge the status quo to deliver better result
  • Transparency and trust are at the core of our business
